    OUR CLASS

    At MMA JUNGLE FITNESS all of our classes include a major focus on MMA, Strength, Conditioning, and Health.

    A small portion of our class is dedicated to the technical aspect of MMA as well as corrective exercises to keep us moving well. We then apply the techniques to conditioning and strength drills. Most of our conditioning and strength drills use the Mixed Martial Arts in a fun but practical way, hitting the bag, grappling with a partner, sparring circuits, etc... Strength/Power training is incorporated in our class through body weight, body manipulation, suspension trainers, med balls, battle ropes, etc...Majority of our classes conclude with some form of sparring, judo, or grappling to keep us moving while finishing the class with the excitement we enjoy.

    Additional to this, students are welcome and encouraged to freely get in additional lifts before class or on Saturdays during our open matt (especially for major lifts such as the squat, or dead-lift if those interest you). During this time students are welcome to use our equipment. We have barbells, a squat rack, pull up bar, sled, resistance bands, kettle bells, free weights, medicine balls, foam rollers, plyo boxes, and open space.

    *members are required to take at least one private lesson before being allowed access to train freely. This is to ensure safety as well as to be introduced to the industry's best methods for lifting and strategy.
