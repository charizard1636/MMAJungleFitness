    OUR PROGRAM

    MMA

    MMA Stands for Mixed Martial Arts. Any combination of standing, taking down, and grappling arts constitutes MMA. In MMA JUNGLE FITNESS we teach directly from the Hoteikan System of Martials Arts which has three major components: Jujitsu, Karate, and Judo. In addition to these major arts, the Hoteikan System has taken the very best of many other arts to form a complete system.

    San Do Ichi Ryu Jujutsu- A Martial Art consisting of joint locks, throwing, and grappling techniques. Translated from Japanese it means "The Gentle Art" ( Ju- gentle, soft and jutsu- art).

    Shotokan Karate- Unarmed combat using the hands and feet to deliver and block blows. Translated from Japanese it means the art of the "Empty Hand".

    Judo- A sport of unarmed combat derived from jujitsu and intended to train the body and mind. It involves using holds and leverage to unbalance and throw the opponent.

    Kickboxing- Although kickboxing is not listed as one of our 3 major arts, it is one of the "other arts" mentioned above in which we incorporate into our system. Kickboxing is based on kicking and punching, historically developed from karate, muay thai, and western boxing.

    At MMA JUNGLE FITNESS we focus on the sport end of these arts to be able to apply them in a fun way, but our origins are from the Hoteikan System of Martial Arts which is also a very real, tough, and practical self defense art. With this, all of our techniques are often referenced to how one could use them in practical self defense if necessary.
