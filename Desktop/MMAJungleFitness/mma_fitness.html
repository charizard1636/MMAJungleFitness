    WHY MMA FITNESS? These are our top 10 specialties.

    1- Your Body - Look good, feel good, and perform well. Skip the gym machines. MMA is functional training at its best. It includes all aspects of health related fitness in one session (cardio, functional strength, and flexibility), and also covers athletic abilities (such as power, balance, speed, and coordination).

    2- The Martial Arts – Train with a purpose and learn from real Martial Artists who know the art well. We are not a “hit the bag aimlessly gym”, we are a dojo. We teach technique and real world practical martial arts. The option of earning your rank under the Hoteikan System is also available for those interested

    3- Health- Our training develops overall wellness including body, mind, and spirit. Our physical training includes corrective exercises, mobility, breathing, and functional movement so you can not only perform well, but also feel good.

    4- Expertise- Our instructors always continue to learn and apply the best of the industry’s knowledge on three ends: the Martial Arts, Strength and Conditioning, and Health.

    5- Enjoyment- Forget the treadmill: run, jump, spar and grapple. Bring fun back to working out. Our training is challenging, but also an exciting experience.

    6- Safety- Safety is a priority! Our training is not for ring competitors; it is geared for the general population. Even though we do spar and grapple, we do so with caution and safety first. It doesn’t make sense to damage your health as you are here to improve it.

    7- Training for life- Our program is designed with health in mind to preserve the body. We enjoy what we do; therefore, we want to do it for as long as we can. We want long term success. It’s like a retirement plan for your body and mind. Invest today, our training lasts forever.

    8- Practical and convenient - Train in your own home, on your own time, and anywhere your heart desires. No equipment is necessary if you want to practice on your own time.

    9- Positive Environment- We are an ego free environment. At MMA Fitness we are more than a club, we are a family.

    10- Healthy hobby- There are too many unhealthy options out there. It’s time to give your spare time to something that’s good for you.
