    Sensei Anthony Carrano

    Trained in the Hoteikan System of Martial Arts since age 3 where he is currently a 3rd degree black belt and ranked in the U.S.J.A (United States Judo Assossiation) as a Nikkyu. Coached, competed, and placed in numerous competitions ranging from AAU Karate Nationals Competition, Judo Collegiate Nationals, Full Contact Kickboxing at the Capitale, Extreme Team MMA, and many local and open tournaments. The Martial Arts has been a lifelong passion, hobby, and way of life for Anthony.

    His journey for Strength and Conidtioning began at Brooklyn College with the Strength and Conditioning program nick named "The Pit" under the direction of Dr. Pat Davidson (current director of training methodology at Peak Performance in NYC). From here Anthony went on to become a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist C.S.C.S with the N.S.C.A (National Strength and Conditioning Association).

    Additional to all of his achievements he has also coached track and field, is a current NYC DOE Physical Education/Health Teacher. and holds a Masters degree in Exercise Science.

    He has attended numerous professional development seminars with teaching tips from the greatest instructors in the world (including the prestigious West Point Military Academy, National Perform Better Summit, and the National Martial Art Super Show held by MIAI).

    It doesn’t end here. As the best teachers know it is important for not only students but also for teachers to continue on their path to education. With this Anthony is currently studying with the Postural Restoration Institute to incorporate physical therapy concepts and strategies to continue to improve our member’s physical health. He is always embarking on the journey to education, staying up to date, applying, and modifying when necessary to keep our program the best it can be.

    Anthony is the founder of the MMA JUNGLE FITNESS program where he teaches under the Hoteikan System of Martial Arts, founded by Grandmaster Robert Hasman and Grandmaster Emilio Claudio, directed by Soke Santiago Armstrong, and overseen by Master Thomas Carrano at the Hoteikan Brooklyn Location.

    Sensei Tom Carrano

    He's an 8th degree blackbelt who began training in the arts at age 11. Shihan Carrano's been teaching the martial arts for over 25 years. He's Hoteikan Dojo's chief instructor in Bayridge Brooklyn and oversees all classes, teaches the Black belt class and the instructors/leadership program.

    Well versed in Karate and Jujitsu, Shihan Carrano is well known and respected by his peers in the martial arts. His love and expertise in the martial arts is evident in his teaching. He teaches all ages and levels and conducts learning in a strict but fun and creative environment. He has instructed and trained winners of Regional, National, and World Championship levels. Not only does Shihan Carrano train students; he himself trains for tournaments and has won many competitions with his flawless Katas and crafty sparring techniques. He's actively involved in the community and volunteers his time helping students in public schools with a bully buster program. He's a licensed Massage Therapist and specializes in Shiatsu. A mechanic by trade where he's applied his skills for over 25 years with the NYC Transit Authority and is presently Director of Subway Safety for TWU Local 100. Shihan Carrano is also the founder of the X-treme Team Martial Arts league, graduate of Cornell's leadership program, a husband, father & grandfather.
