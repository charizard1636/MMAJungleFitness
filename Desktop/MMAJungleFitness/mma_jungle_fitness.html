    WHY WE CALL OURSELVES MMAJUNGLEFITNESS

    I was once asked the question, "why is the Tiger the king of the jungle?" (yes the Tiger, not the Lion, Lions mainly live in the savannah :) ) He is not the strongest, nor is he the fastest, he is not the top in any given ability, so how is it that he is considered the KING?

    Before reading any further look away and try to answer the question yourself, see what you come up with.

    After much thought, I was given the response. He may not be the strongest, he may not be the fastest, he may not have the best endurance, but he is equally balanced in all abilities.

    When an athlete becomes the greatest in one ability, he also sacrifices other abilities to get there.Unlike most athletes the Tiger remains equally balanced in all. He is fast enough, strong enough, powerful enough and can last long enough to remain top of the food chain, thus making him KING OF THE JUNGLE.

    At MMA JUNGLE FITNESS our goal is to become the Tiger; equally balanced in all abilities. As we know, our main goal at THE JUNGLE is not performance, but rather HEALTH ! But remember the same abilities that make the Tiger KING OF THE JUNGLE are the same abilities that we need to be healthy. They are the same abilities that allow us to catch ourselves from a fall, or even carry groceries home from the store, the only difference is that we need them on a smaller scale.

    A body in balance is a healthy body. The Tiger is symbolic to a balanced body; not amazing in one, but rather equally sufficient in all abilities. This approach to balance is one of our main philosophies behind our Strength and Conditioning program at THE JUNGLE. It is this approach that will help to create lifelong participants with short term and long term health in mind.

    This approach to balance in our Strength and Conditioning program also happens to coincide perfectly with the MMA participant since they too must have balance in all the abilities. The speed to strike, the power to throw, the strength to grapple, the flexibility to kick, and the conditioning/cardio to last. The MMA participant will train all abilities in a balanced, fun, functional way.

    The MMA in our name is clearly dedicated to the Martial Arts and the Hoteikan System of Martial Arts

    The JUNGLE FITNESS is dedicated to the Strength and Conditioning portion of our training

    Together you find MMA JUNGLE FITNESS.
