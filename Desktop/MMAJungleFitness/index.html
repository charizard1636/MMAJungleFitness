    ABOUT ME:

    Our program is designed for the general population who are looking to get in great shape and learn the arts. Our program is designed to use mixed martial arts training and strength and conditioning concepts as a venue for improving ones overall health.

    We pride ourselves in Health, Enjoyment, Strength and Conditioning, and the Martial Arts.

    MISSION:

    Our goal is to give members more than just a club membership, but rather a hobby they can carry with them for the rest of their lives, a practical means of staying in shape, a lifestyle change for their health, and the enjoyment to keep coming back. Part of this goal includes exposing the benefits of the martial arts and strength and conditioning as a great tool for accomplishing this, especially when taught with our unique style.
